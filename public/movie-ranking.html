The Best Movies Ever Made

According to Alex

Gladiator

Russell Crowe is legendary in this one. Very quotable, great music, and story that is overwhelmingly human amidst such grandeur

Italian Job (Newer)

Honestly, just a fun, exciting watch. Cast is great and the story is perfectly self aware but sincere.

Dune

If anything, it's the music for me. It's audibly and visually stunning.
